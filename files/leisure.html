Fitness

I'm an ardent fitness enthusiast. To me, fitness is more than an hour of working out in a day- it's a lifestyle . Fitness is a discipline and a way of life. It teaches us the importance of commitment, perseverance, pursuing progress, and taking pride in our hard work .

Most importantly, it teaches us to set goals , go the extra mile , fight strong , and to rest but never to quit . Pain lies outside our comfort zone, but so does success doesn't it? Fitness doesn't just get our bodies in shape, it gets our mind in shape because life begins at the end of our comfort zone .
